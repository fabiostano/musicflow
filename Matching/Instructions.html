Scenario:

Your company has decided to digitize the employee information database.

For that reason, interns have already transcribed all the information form paper form into your software system.

To avoid mistakes, all entries have been transcribed twice by the interns.

Your task will be to check whether all these duplicates match, and find where there is a mismatch.

Task:

Over the next 10 minutes, you will be presented with pairs of filled out forms, which need to be checked for matches.

For each pair, it is your task to check whether the content of the forms is the same or differs in one of the fields.
