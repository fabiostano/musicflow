Thank you for participating in this study. Please read the following instructions carefully.

In this study, you will be presented with samples of songs, which you are asked to rate. You should wear headphones or use decent speakers, to ensure good audio quality.

There are no right or wrong answers in this study - we are only interested in your personal opinion.

The study will take approximately 60 minutes.

If you want to receive your payout, you will be prompted to give your bank details in the end of the experiment. This data will be stored separately from the rest to ensure anonymity. Immediately after processing your payout, we will delete your data.

If you have any questions about the study please contact Fabio Stano (fabio.stano@kit.edu) at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT).
