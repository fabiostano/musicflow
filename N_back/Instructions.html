Scenario:

You just came back to work from a vacation, and you email inbox is full of unread mail.

Since there was a bug in the IT System of your company, some mails have been delivered twice.

To make things easier, you decide to first go through all the subject lines and delete duplicates.

Task:

Over the next 10 minutes, your task will be to go through email subject lines one by one and decide whether it is a duplicate or not.

A mail is considered a duplicate, if the mail directly before had the exact same subject line.

You don't have to remember all the other mails, and only check for each mail if the current subject line is the same as the one before.
